Imaging planets from imaginary worlds

Hollis B. Akins, Donald A. Smith
September 2018
Project Poster DOI

Abstract

For my final project for an observational astronomy class at Guilford College, I used a method called Lucky Imaging to photograph planets and incoorporate them into artistic photographs. The project was exhibited as a poster presentation at the Guilford Undergraduate Symposium, and turned into an article which was published in the October 2018 edition of The Physics Teacher. Both the poster and article can be found here.
