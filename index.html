About me

Hello there! I am an undergraduate student currently studying physics at Grinnell College in Grinnell, IA. There I work as mentor at the Data Analysis and Social Inquiry Lab and participate in astrophysics research with the Physics Department in collaboration with the N-Body Shop. My primary research interests are in computational and data-intensive astrophysics and cosmology, and I am pursuing research opportunities in these fields. I am also an avid photographer and enjoy exploring art, design, architecture, and urban planning.

This personal website is intended to showcase both astrophysics research projects and other artistic/academic endeavors.
